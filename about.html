Our Story

Built from
Frustration.
Driven by Pride.

Nepal has brilliant engineers. What it hasn't had — until now — is a tech company that holds itself to the same standard as the best firms in the world. That's the gap NTS exists to close.

Founded in February 2025 by Shivam Pajiyar in Imadol, Lalitpur, Nepal Technology Service was built on a simple belief: geography shouldn't determine the quality of software your business gets.

The western tech world has spent decades developing best practices — in security, in DevOps, in design, in engineering culture. We've studied those standards deeply. Now we're bringing them home.

We're young, we're GenZ, and we're unapologetically ambitious. We don't want to be the best IT company in Nepal. We want to be a company that just happens to be from Nepal and competes globally.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Not just words on a wall. These are the actual principles that shape every decision we make.

01

International Standards. Always.

We don't benchmark ourselves against local competitors. We benchmark against the best in the world — then we try to beat them.

02

Ship Real Things.

No endless planning. No over-engineering. We build, we ship, we iterate. Real products in the hands of real users — that's the goal.

03

Security Isn't Optional.

Every system we build is designed with security from day one. Not bolted on at the end. Not a checkbox. Built in.

04

AI as a Tool, Not a Crutch.

We embrace AI to move faster and think better. But we never let it replace judgment, craft, or accountability.

05

Stay Current or Get Left Behind.

Technology doesn't wait. Neither do we. Continuous learning isn't a nice-to-have — it's a core job requirement at NTS.

06

Proudly From Nepal.

We're not trying to hide where we're from. We're trying to make where we're from something to be proud of in tech.
