The Story

THE GAP WAS
TOO OBVIOUS
TO IGNORE.

Nepal has talented engineers. That's never been the problem. The problem is the environment — most developers here have never worked inside a team that does proper code reviews, runs automated test suites, maintains real documentation, or treats security as a design requirement rather than an afterthought.

The result is a market full of "it works, doesn't it?" software. Which is fine, until it isn't. Until the client scales, or gets breached, or tries to hand the codebase to a new team, or wants to raise investment from people who run technical due diligence.

Nepal Technology Service was founded in February 2025 to close that gap — to be the company that holds itself to international standards from day one, not because clients demand it, but because we know what properly-built software looks like and we won't ship anything less.

We're based in Imadol, Lalitpur. We're GenZ. We're two years in. We've already shipped real products to production. And we're nowhere near done.

What We Stand For

SIX VALUES.
NON-NEGOTIABLE.

These aren't marketing copy. They're the actual decisions we make every day when we choose how to build something.

01

International Standards. Always.

We don't benchmark against local competitors. We benchmark against the best teams in the world — then we try to build better than them.

02

Ship Real Things.

No endless planning cycles. No over-engineering for problems that don't exist. Build, ship, iterate. Real products in real users' hands.

03

Security Is Not Optional.

Every system we build is designed with security as a requirement from the first line of code. Not reviewed at the end. Not a feature tier. Built in.

04

AI as a Multiplier, Not a Replacement.

We use AI tools across our entire workflow. We use them to move faster and eliminate repetitive work — not to replace engineering judgment or accountability.

05

Stay Current or Get Left Behind.

Technology moves. We move with it. Continuous learning is a core part of the job at NTS, not something that happens in spare time.

06

Proudly From Nepal.

We're not trying to hide where we're from or pretend to be a Western agency. We're trying to make Nepal something worth pointing to in tech.
